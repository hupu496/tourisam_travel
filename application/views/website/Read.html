The Holy Land tour Tours

Day 1:

Explore Madaba and Mt. Nebo, with dinner and an overnight stay in Amman.

Day 2:

Visit Nazareth, including Mary’s Well, the Greek Orthodox Church of Annunciation, the Catholic Basilica of the Annunciation, St. Joseph’s Church (the site of Joseph’s workshop), and Cana (the first Miracle Church). Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay in Nazareth or Tiberias.

Day 3:

Experience a boat ride on the Sea of Galilee, visit Yardenit (the river Jordan), Mt. Beatitudes, the Church of Multiplication of Loaves and Fishes, Peter’s Primacy Church, Capernaum, St. Peter’s House and the Ancient Synagogue, and Mt. Tabor. Dinner and overnight stay will be in Nazareth or Tiberias.

Day 4:

Day 5:

Ascend Mt. of Olives and visit key holy sites including Gethsemane, Mt. Zion, Ein Karem, and the Western Wall, followed by dinner and overnight in Bethlehem.

Day 6:

Explore Jerusalem’s Bethesda Pool, the birthplace of the Blessed Mary, the Flagellation Monastery, the site of the Antonio fortress where Jesus was condemned to death, Via Dolorosa, Calvary, Holy Sepulcher Church, Bethany (place of Lazarus, Martha, and Mary), Jericho, Mount Temptation, the Sycamore tree, and the Dead Sea. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay in Bethlehem.

Day 7:

Cross the Taba border to Egypt, drive thru the Sinai Peninsula and night halt at Hotel.

Day 8:

Ascend the mountain of Sinai, visit the monastery of St. Catherine and the burning bush, then travel via the Suez Canal to Cairo for an overnight stay.

Day 9:

Discover the Pyramids, the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Sphinx, Pharaonic Village and Coptic Churches, and enjoy a Nile Cruise dinner. Overnight in Cairo

Day 10:

Depart from Cairo.
