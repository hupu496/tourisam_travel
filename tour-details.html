Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Madaba and Mt. Nebo

Arrival to Queen Alia Airport in Amman, meeting by our Jordanian Representative, drive to visit Madaba (Numbers 21:30 and Joshua 13:9) but best known for the oldest extant map of the Holy Land, and then to Mt. Nebo (Deuteronomy 32:48-52). Dinner and overnight in Amman.

Amman Dinner
Day 2

Nazareth

Morning transfer from Amman to Sheikh Hussein Bridge to cross the border. Enter Israel and travel to Nazareth. Visit Mary’s Well, Greek Orthodox Church of Annunciation, Catholic Basilica of the Annunciation (Lk. 1, 26-38), St. Joseph’s Church, the place of Joseph’s workshop (Lk 2: 41-52), continue to Cana Holy Mass at the first Miracle Church (Jn 2: 1-12). Dinner and overnight in Nazareth or Tiberias.

Nazareth or Tiberias Dinner
Day 3

Sea of Galilee, Yardenit or Mt. Beatitudes

Morning boat ride on the Sea of Galilee (Mt 8:23-27; Mk 4:35-40; Lk 8:22-25; Mt. 14 22-27; Mk 6: 45-52; Jn 6: 16-21), proceed to Yardenit, renewal of Baptism in river Jordan (Mk 1: 9-11; Mt. 3: 13-17, Lk3: 2122) drive to Mt. Beatitudes (Mt 5-7), Church of Multiplication of Loaves and Fishes (Jn. 6: 1-15; Mt. 14: 13-21; Lk. 9: 10-17; Mk 6: 30-44), Peter’s Primacy Church (Jn 21: 1-19), Capernaum, St. Peter’s House and Ancient Synagogue (Mk. 1: 21-3:12 and parallels; Mt. 17: 24-27; Jn 6: 25-59). Ascend Mt. Tabor (Mt. 17: 1- 8; Mk 9:2-8; Lk. 9: 28-36; 2 Peter 1, 16-21). Dinner and overnight in Nazareth or Tiberias.

Nazareth or Tiberias Dinner
Days 4-5

Bethlehem

Drive to Haifa, Israel’s largest port city, visit Mt. Carmel and the Bahai Gardens and proceed to Bethlehem. Visit the Shepherd’s Fields, the grotto of the Shepherds, Milk Grotto, Holy Mass at Bethlehem, Nativity Church birth place of Jesus (Mt 1:18-25; Lk 2: 1-7). Dinner and overnight in Bethlehem. Morning drive to Mt. of Olives, Shrine of Ascension (Acts 1: 6-11); visit Pater Noster, the place where Jesus taught Our Father (Lk 11, 2-4; Mt. 24), visit Dominus Flevit (Mt. 23: 33-37//); Continue to Garden of Gethsemane, Basilica of Agony, Grotto of Gethsemane (Lk.22//) Tomb of Bl. Virgin Mary. Continue to Mt. Zion, visit the Room of the Last Supper, Tomb of King David, St. Peter’s Church Gallicantu (Mt 26 and parallels), and proceed to Ein Karem, birthplace of John the Baptist. Evening visit the Western Wall. Dinner and overnight in Bethlehem.

Bethlehem Dinner
Days 6-7

Jerusalem’s Bethesda Pool or Sinai Peninsula

Morning drive to Jerusalem visit Bethesda Pool (Jn 5:1-12), and birth place of Blessed Mary, Flagellation Monastery, the site of Antonio fortress where Jesus was condemned to death (Mk 15: 1- 15; LK 23: 3-25; Mt 27: 11-30), Way of the Cross through Via Dolorosa , Calvary, Holy Sepulcher Church, Holy Mass at the Holy Sepulcher Church, drive to Bethany, Place of Lazarus, Martha and Mary (John 11- 12); proceed to Jericho, view Mount Temptation (Lk 4: 1-13), visit Sycamore tree (Lk 19: 1-4),and the Dead Sea. Return to Bethlehem for dinner and overnight in Bethlehem. After breakfast transfer from Bethlehem to Taba border for departure to Sinai. Meet and assist by the Egyptian representative and drive through Sinai to St. Catherine to your hotel for dinner and overnight.

Bethlehem Breakfast or Dinner
Days 8-9

mountain of Sinai or The Great Pyramid of Giza

Early in the morning climb the mountain of Sinai, then have your breakfast and visit the monastery of St. Catherine. Set beneath the mountain where Moses received the Ten commandments. St Catherine Monastery is one of the world’s great religious pilgrimages from 15th centuries. Drive to Cairo, on the way we cross the famous Suez Canal to then to your hotel for overnight. After breakfast visit the Pyramids. The great Pyramid of Giza ( also called the Pyramid of Khufu and the Pyramid of Cheops) is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in Giza. It is believed that the Pyramids were built as a tomb for the Fourth Dynasty Egyptian Pharaoh Khufu and constructed over 20 years . The tallest man made structure in the world Sphinx is over 3800 years old. Then visit the Pharaonic Village on the boat , Lunch , Visit to the Coptic Church. Evening Nile Cruise and have dinner on the Cruise.

Cairo Breakfast, Farewell Dinner
Day 10

Departure

Departure from Cairo and Arrival at Delhi.
